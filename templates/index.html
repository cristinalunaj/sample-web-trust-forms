Trustworthiness Annotations

Thank you very much for your cooperation!
This is an anonymous survey that would take no more than 20 minutes to complete. We are a team of multinational researchers studying the effects of TRUST during a brief intervention at zero acquaintance.
During the survey, you will be asked about several physical and psychological aspects of the person that appear in the videos (including attractiveness, emotion, trustworthiness perceived, honesty...)
, in which you should give an answer in the range from -3 (Extremely Negative) to +3 (Extremely Positive).
It is important that you rate each person in the videos objectively - specifically, HOW the information is expressed (the behaviour/action), and not what it is said (the content of the message).
